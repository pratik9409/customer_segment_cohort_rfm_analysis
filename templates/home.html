📊 Customer Analytics Dashboard

Gain insights into customer behavior, segmentation, and predictions to drive business growth.

🔍 Project Overview

This platform provides a comprehensive analysis of customer purchasing patterns, helping businesses segment their customers, predict churn, forecast sales, and detect anomalies. The system is built using Flask, PostgreSQL, and various machine learning models.

🚀 Key Features

  • 📌 Customer Segmentation: Uses RFM analysis and K-Means clustering to categorize customers based on their purchasing behavior.
  • 📅 Cohort Analysis: Tracks customer retention and engagement trends over time.
  • 🌍 Sales by Country: Analyzes sales distribution across different regions.
  • 💰 Customer Lifetime Value (LTV): Estimates the total revenue a business can expect from a customer.
  • ⚠️ Churn Prediction: Identifies customers at risk of leaving based on purchasing inactivity.
  • 📈 Sales Forecasting: Uses time-series forecasting techniques to predict future sales trends.
  • 🔍 Anomaly Detection: Detects unusual purchasing patterns to flag potential fraud or errors.
  • 🛢️ Database Integration: Stores customer transaction data in PostgreSQL, ensuring scalability and efficient querying.
  • 📊 Interactive Dashboard: Provides easy navigation to different analytical sections.
